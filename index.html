Features

  • Public assets

    Public assets are creations taken from both the community and Renbusi developers that can be imported and edited in any private marketing plan. These assets include Strategies (Campaigns), Competitors (SWOT), and Buyer Profiles.

  • Multiple Outputs

    With both PDF and Word DOCX output available users can export plans how they see fit. Want a preformatted PDF? Done. Need a little creative control over the document? We have you covered. Both files are well formatted and easy to understand, and although we're always improving them, we're sure you can go straight to your board.

  • Benchmarking

    Automated benchmarking can be misleading, even marketing automation suites do not have the ability to benchmark all activities. A fully realized marketing plan has the ability to benchmark results against cost. Renbusi allows marketers to benchmark the entireity of a plan.

  • Standalone SWOT

    If a competitive analysis (SWOT) is what you need, you're in the right place. Renbusi offers a standalone output of competitors, creating a detailed SWOT analysis without any extra marketing plan features.
